DISCOVERING ARTISTIC INSPIRATIONS

One of the most difficult things as a visual designer is understanding your design style. We often run into creative slumps
that prevent our creativity to flourish. During those moments, the best thing to do is to refer to other people's work for
inspiration. However, it is also important to pay attention to your own style and find inspiration that will move it in the
right direction. I've been a visual designer for 5 years. During those years, I put a lot of effort into exploring visual styles
and techniques. I realized that my design style is catered to minimalism and typography. I work a lot with a monochromatic
color palette to visualize my imagery. Creating sleek and clean work is something I thoroughly enjoy doing. So when I
experience a creative slump, I am researching other minimalist work to create a spark in me.
